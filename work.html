Previous Work

I worked as a research assistant in the summer of 2019 with Dr. Ryan Hayward attempting to solve the game "Hex" on a 10x10 board

I worked for Dr. Yvonne Wong designing a new system to be used by kids ages 4-6 who have ADHD, ADD, Autism, or any other similar disorder. This included a mobile game that connects with a Muse headset, as well as an online dashboard that stores brainwave information and allows game parameters to be changed. The purpose of the app is to provide an affordable solution to the expensive treatments that often come along with ADHD, Autism, etc.


Volunteering

I am president of East Campus Dodgeball League at the University of Alberta. We organize games, tournaments, and other events for our community of over 200 members.

I've volunteered with Mossom Creek Hatchery since 2012, where we raised Pink, Coho, and Chum salmon from eggs. I helped feed the fish, clean the tanks, remove invasive plant species, and many other things.
